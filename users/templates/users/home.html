Baseball Management System (BMS)

Baseball Management System is a software as a service (SaaS) that will provide your baseball organization with tools to help you manage the day to day operations of manage an single baseball club or a collection of baseball clubs.

BMS provides the following tools to help meet your needs for managing any baseball club(s):

  • Manage your teams communication and collobration through an internal portal. Each team gets:
    • Each team will recieve public website along with a content management system to allow adminstrators to manage teams website content.
    • Manage player's stats and tracking progress (Comming Soon!!!)
    • Each team will be equiped with a secure messenger app that authorized users can use to talk to each other in real time.
    • Game Tracker App that will allow fans to watch live games. (Comming Soon !!!)
    • Stats Search engine for users to lookup players stats. (Comming Soon !!!)
